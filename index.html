      Password Security Tips

      01

      Use a Passphrase

      Combine 4–5 random words. "correct-horse-battery-staple" is long, memorable, and very hard to crack.

      02

      Never Reuse Passwords

      If one site is breached, attackers try your password everywhere. Use a password manager.

      03

      Enable 2FA

      Two-factor authentication adds a second layer. Even if your password leaks, accounts stay safe.

      04

      Avoid Personal Info

      Names, birthdays, and pet names are easy for attackers to guess using social engineering.

      05

      Check for Breaches

      Use services like HaveIBeenPwned to see if your email/password appeared in known data breaches.

      06

      Change Regularly

      Update passwords every 6–12 months, especially for sensitive accounts like email and banking.
